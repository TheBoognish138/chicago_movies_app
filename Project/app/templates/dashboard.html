Chicago: Movies in the Park

A free initiative through the Chicago Parks District, "Movies in the Parks" gives Chicago residents and visitors the opportunity to see some of their favorite films outdoors on the big screen. Data from 2014 to 2019 is publicly available, except for 2016, in which dates of showing were not logged. Check out this dashboard to see which movies were most common for each month across years, as well as on which days of the week film showings most often occurred. Note that films with only one showing are automatically filtered out of the bar chart.
