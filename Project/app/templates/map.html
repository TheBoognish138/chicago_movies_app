Chicago: Movies in the Park

Envisioning a fun, free night out in Chicago? Check out where Chicago has hosted free movie showings between 2014 and 2019. Markers indicate every showing recorded which can be filtered by year.

Bringing the whole family? Each community area is shaded to demonstrate how common showings of G and PG movies were. Communities marked as "Rare" did have at least some film showings within the years filtered, but family friendly films in a neighborhood shaded "Periodic" are played twice as often, three times as often in a neighborhood shaded "Regular", and the most common in the "Frequent" neighborhoods.
